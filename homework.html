Homework Logistics

  • Submission: Homework is due each Wednesday in section, by 20 minutes after the hour. (i.e. If your section starts at 3 pm, you must hand in your homework by 3.20 pm at the latest.) Late submissions will not be accepted.

    Your homework must have your name, SID number, discussion section number and assignment number clearly written at the top; additionally, if required, please staple your homework before submitting it.

  • Grading: Papers will be graded on a 0/1 scale. Each assignment will consist of 8 problems. A good attempt at 6 or more problems will receive the score of 1; anything else will get 0.
