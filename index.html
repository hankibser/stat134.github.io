Math 104: Introduction to Analysis (Fall '16)

Instructor:

Marina Iliopoulou

Time/Location:

MWF 3:00 pm - 4:00 pm, 241 Cory

Office Hours:

Tu 4:00 pm - 6:00 pm, Th 11:00 am - 1:00 pm, 849 Evans

Description:

Real numbers, sequences, series, limits, continuity, differentiability, mean value theorem, uniform convergence, interchange of limit operations, basics of metric spaces, Riemann integration.

Prerequisites:

Math 53 (Multivariable Calculus), Math 54 (Linear Algebra and Differential Equations)

Textbook:

Principals of Mathematical Analysis (3rd Edition), Walter Rudin

Supplemental Texts:

Calculus (4th Edition), Michael Spivak

Exams:

  • Midterm: 10 October, 3:00 - 4:00 pm, 241 Cory

  • Final: 13 December, 7:00 - 10:00 pm, location TBA

  • (All exams are closed book.)

Grading:

  • 20% Assignments, 30% Midterm, 50% Final

  • (The lowest score from the weekly assignments will be dropped.)
